A bit about Fortis

Here you will find a little insight in our growing business

Hi there! We're glad to see you're interested in us. We welcome you to our business. We started Fortis this year (2024) and are based in the heart of Wales, Cardiff! We love what we do and hope you will too!

Our mission

We are a website development company solely aimed at helping businesses within the trade industry. We believe there is a huge advantage for tradespeople to get into the online world of their business and not abandon it like most businesses in the same industry do. We completely understand that you may not have the time, as you have a business to run, of course. So, at Fortis, our whole mission is giving trade businesses the advantage of an online platform that can guide customers to do what you want (most of the time requesting a quote), share more about your business, build trust and credibility with a capable brand, and leave you without any stress and worry regarding the online side of your business.

Our values

It has taken our team members years to acquire their skills, and with how busy you will be running a business, it will be hard work trying to balance everything yourself. We want to help your business thrive, not complicate it, which is why we are available round the clock, 24/7. Our business is based on building a long-term relationship with our customers, not a "build the website and forget about you" approach. We love our work and believe we can help any trade supercharge their business. We have worked with many people this year and have received great feedback from our customers.
